Music:Classical Piano

by Natasha Audrey Lukmanto

My journey as a classical pianist starts far back at 2008 and I've been finding joy everytime I play classical piano to this very second. Playing the piano has always been and will always be one of my hobbies. I do find mastering new pieces is challenging enough to keep me entertained for a very long time, but also a feeling of supreme satisfaction after each and every piece I master. It's a feeling I can't ever get enough of. On the other hand, I think classical pieces bring peace and a sense of calm when it rings through my ears. I won't ever stop playing piano!

I learn piano under the guidance of remarkable music teachers through formal education. I started with Yamaha Music Education as a formal music school institution from the beginning. Here are my achievement through my years of studying in my music school:

  • Passed KMA 1 under Nuansa Musik Yayasan Musik Indonesia Institution. Date: 14 December 2008.
  • Passed KMA 2 under Nuansa Musik Yayasan Musik Indonesia Institution. Date: 31 May 2009.
  • Passed KMA 3 under Nuansa Musik Yayasan Musik Indonesia Institution. Date: 10 January 2010.
  • Passed Junior Music Course Fundamental Skills Survey under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 11 July 2010.
  • Passed Piano Grade 10 with score: 89/100 under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 12 June 2011.
  • Passed Piano Grade 9 with score: 88/100 under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 10 June 2012.
  • Passed Piano Grade 8 with score: 91/100 under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 18 May 2014.
  • Passed Piano Grade 7 with score: 89/100 under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 17 July 2016.
  • Participated in Junior Original Concert under Yamaha Music Foundation with original composition. Date: 2017-2019
  • Participated in Music My Friend: Ensemble Concert under Yamaha Music Foundation. Date: 2017-2019
